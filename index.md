# Open Science at LiU   
Read more and understand how LiU encourages and supports *Open and Reproducible Research Practices*  
* [Open Science Policy](https://styrdokument.liu.se/Regelsamling/VisaBeslut/RS-2025-00005) - read about the policy surrounding open data, data management plans and open access publications 
* [CoARA Action Plan](https://zenodo.org/records/16030775) -  read about LiU's efforts towards open, transparent and fair reform of the merit systems of achievements and competences of academic personnel
* [Library support](https://liu.se/en/article/forskningsstod) - how the library personnel can support you in implementing open science practices, such as sharing data and perparing your data management plans 
* [PhD Course on open science](https://liu.se/forskning/forskarutbildning/kurser-pa-forskarniva/open-science-for-phd-students) - what open science training is currently available
